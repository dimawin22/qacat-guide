Linguist quick start

LQT guide for linguists

Everything you need on QACAT — clear steps, screenshots, and a short video.

Upload → Mark → Fix → Done
1 Access · 2 Task · 3 Upload · 4 Review · 5 Fixed · 6 Send · 7 Video · 8 Support

1 Registration & access

If you are not registered yet:

  • Open the invite link you received
  • Create your account
  • Join the Alconost workspace

After that, your assigned LQT report appears on your dashboard.

2 Your task

  1. Open the assigned LQT report
  2. Upload screenshots
  3. Review them and mark issues

3 Upload screenshots

  • Use the + button or drag and drop files
  • If you upload many images, wait until loading has finished

4 Review & mark issues

  • Click a screenshot
  • Draw a box (marker) around the problem
  • Add a short comment explaining the issue

Optional · Use OCR Trigger if you want to pull text from the screenshot.

!

Alerts: If an issue is incomplete (required fields empty), fix the fields or remove the issue.

5 Fix the issue & Fixed status

For each issue:

  • Enter the correct wording in Fixed translation
  • Add a QA comment if needed (e.g. what was wrong)
  • If the issue is resolved in the project, turn on Fixed
  • If you cannot fix it, leave a short comment with your reasoning (do not mark as Fixed)

Before delivery: When all issues are created, the Fixed status appears in the QA table in the report. Review that table and make sure every issue is truly fixed in the project. If something stays open, explain why briefly in the comment.

6 Send review & updates

When your review is ready, click Send review.

This will:

  • notify the project manager that your review is available
  • share the current version of your findings
  • mark the report as submitted

You can continue editing the report after sending it.

If you make additional changes later, click Send update to notify the team again.

Use Send update when you:

  • add new issues
  • update translations
  • upload additional screenshots
  • improve comments or fixes

The report always remains editable — sending the review does NOT lock your work.
